Vilius' khlav kalash


Back to all recipes

My friend made this one when camping, and it was really good, and so i made it.

Tastes really good and costs actual pennies!

Ingredients

  • 125g bag of rice
  • 1 pack of smoked sausages
  • 1-2 onions
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 tomato
  • salt, pepper, paprika, ketchup

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan, add onions & garlic
  2. Add tomatoes
  3. Add sausage, stir thoroughly
  4. Add boiled rice
  5. Add corn, spices, stir til little moisture left
  6. Add spices, serve
