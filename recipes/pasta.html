It's pasta with pasta sauce and cheese on it, this is the bottom of the barrel.

At least it costs nothing and you can make tons of it.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of pasta
  • 1 can of pasta sauce
  • 1/2 bag of shredded cheese
  • salt, pepper, paprika, ketchup

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Add sauce
  3. Add spices
  4. Add cheese
  5. Put in bowl
  6. Add more cheese on top
